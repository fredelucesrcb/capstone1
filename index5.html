In Pop Culture

the GTR-34 in the Fast and the Furious Franchise

Brian O'Connor's (played by Paul Walker) car of choice is the Nissan Skyline GTR-34. He drove his own personal Skyline during the shooting of 2 Fast 2 Furious. The appearance of the car in the franchise greatly boosted its status as a legend

the GTR-34 in Various Racing games

Numerous racing games, particulary the Need for Speed Franchise, have featured different variants of the Skyline, with the V-Spec being the most popular among players.

The Tuner and Drag Racing Scene

Tuners have been increasingly interested in modifying the components of the Skyline GTR lineage due to their familiraity of the RB26DETT engine the R34 and R33 possess.
